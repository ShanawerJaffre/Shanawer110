INSTAGRAM

Instagram is a social media platform primarily focused on sharing photos and videos. It was launched in 2010 by Kevin Systrom and Mike Krieger, and it quickly gained popularity due to its simple interface and visually-driven content. Users can upload images and videos, apply filters, and share them with their followers. Instagram also supports features like Stories (temporary posts that disappear after 24 hours), Direct Messaging, Reels (short-form videos), and IGTV (longer videos). The platform is often used by individuals, brands, influencers, and creators to showcase their lives, products, or services, and to engage with their audiences through likes, comments, and shares. Instagram was acquired by Facebook (now Meta) in 2012, and it has continued to evolve, adding new features like shopping, live streaming, and AI-driven recommendations. Today, Instagram is one of the most popular social media apps worldwide.
